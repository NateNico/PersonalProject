Learn how you can support the Amhara people in their fight for justice, land restoration, and sovereignty.

Ways You Can Help

1. Donate to the Cause

Your contributions can make a direct impact. Donations go toward humanitarian relief, legal efforts to protect Amhara rights, and supporting displaced families. Click below to donate now.

  • Donate Now

2. Volunteer Your Time

If you're passionate about helping the Amhara people, there are various volunteer opportunities available. From organizing protests to supporting educational initiatives, your involvement can make a difference.

  • Volunteer with Us

3. Raise Awareness

Help spread the word about the ongoing genocide and struggles of the Amhara people. Share their stories on social media, organize local events, and get your community involved in the movement.

  • Learn More

4. Advocate for Policy Change

Contact your local representatives and urge them to take action in addressing the Amhara crisis. Advocate for international intervention, economic sanctions, and human rights protections for the Amhara people.
